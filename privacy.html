Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 31, 2025 · Last updated: December 31, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Two Ducks collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Two Ducks app and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

1) Who we are

Two Ducks is operated by Prathap Kasina. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at team@twoducksapp.com.

2) Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to operate the Service:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect a unique user identifier. If you sign in with Apple, we may receive your name and an email address (which may be a private relay email, depending on your Apple settings). If you sign in with email, we collect the email address you provide for authentication and account communication. (or a private relay email).
  • Pairing information: Anonymous pairing identifiers and relationship status needed to connect you with your partner.
  • App activity: Signals such as whether a match occurred, timestamps, and basic usage events required for app functionality.

3) What we do NOT collect

We do not collect or store messages, photos, audio, contacts, location data, health data, or intimate content.

4) How we use information

  • To operate and improve the Service
  • To enable partner matching and notifications
  • To provide support and respond to inquiries
  • To maintain safety, security, and fraud prevention

5) How information is shared

We do not sell your data. Information is shared only:

  • With your paired partner, strictly as required for the Service
  • With service providers (such as Firebase) that help us operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect users’ safety

6) Data storage and security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure (including Google Firebase / Firestore), with access controls and encryption in transit.

7) Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8) Your choices

  • You can control what information you share through Apple’s Sign in settings
  • You may stop using the Service at any time
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us

9) Children’s privacy

Two Ducks is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent version.

11) Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at team@twoducksapp.com.
