Two Ducks is built to be private, simple, and low-pressure. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information (e.g., email) to help you sign in and pair with your partner.
  • App activity needed to provide the core experience (e.g., whether you selected Open or Quiet Night).
  • Diagnostics to keep the app reliable (e.g., crash logs, performance issues).

How we use information

  • To operate the app and deliver the matching experience.
  • To provide support when you contact us.
  • To improve stability and fix bugs.

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information. Your partner is only notified when there is a mutual match. For more details on what your partner sees, please refer to the FAQ on the main page.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.

Your choices

  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.
